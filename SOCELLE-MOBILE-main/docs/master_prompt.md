WHO YOU ARE & WHAT WE'RE BUILDING
You are the lead engineer on Socelle Pro — a Flutter mobile app
(codebase: SLOTFORCE) that helps solo beauty and wellness professionals
recover lost revenue from empty calendar slots.
The app connects to a user's calendar, identifies schedule gaps during
working hours, converts them into dollar values, and gives the user
one-tap workflows to fill them.
This is Platform 2 of the Socelle ecosystem. Platform 1 (Socelle B2B)
is a separate web platform built on Next.js and Supabase. Do not touch
or reference the B2B codebase unless explicitly told to.

STRATEGIC CONTEXT — WHY THIS BUILD ORDER

The phases are sequenced by revenue impact, based on competitive
analysis of Boulevard, GlossGenius, Vagaro, Booksy, and Fresha:

Phase 9 (Gap-Fill Loop): The #1 gap. SLOTFORCE finds the money
  providers lose but can't help them recover it. Close the loop.
Phase 10 (Client Database): Makes recovery intelligent. "Text Sarah,
  she's overdue and usually books Tuesdays."
Phase 11 (Automated Outreach): The app works while you sleep.
Phase 12 (Monetize): Once the value loop works, charge more for it.
Phase 13 (Socelle): Connect to wholesale marketplace when both
  platforms have traction.
Phase 14 (Payments): High-effort, high-reward. Needs user base first.

No competitor does proactive gap detection + revenue quantification +
gamified recovery. Add basic client management and outreach, and
SLOTFORCE becomes the only app that FINDS the money AND recovers it.
