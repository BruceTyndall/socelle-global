# SOCELLE Copy Audit — Intelligence-First Language Authority

**Audit Date:** March 5, 2026  
**Auditor:** Agent 2 — Copy + Messaging  
**Baseline:** SOCELLE-WEB platform instruction document

## Executive Summary

**Overall Platform Health:** 8.5/10

The SOCELLE platform demonstrates **excellent** intelligence-first positioning and avoids most SaaS clichés. Copy is operator-native, data-forward, and specific to professional beauty context. No generic "unlock growth" language detected.

**Key Strengths:**
- H1s and meta titles consistently reference intelligence, signals, benchmarks, and market positioning
- Zero use of banned phrases like "seamless," "leverage," "empower," "synergy"
- Vertical marketing (ForMedspas, ForSalons) is clinically and operationally accurate
- CTAs are action-oriented (Request Access, Explore Intelligence, Browse Marketplace)
- All pages have proper Helmet/meta tags

**Minor Improvements:**
- 3 instances of "real-time analytics" (should be "signals" or "market intelligence")
- 2 instances of "data-driven insights" (should be more specific)
- A few CTAs could be more directive (e.g., "View all signals" → "See trending signals")
